Century Plant Care Guide

Soil for Century Plant

Well-Draining Soil

Use sandy, well-draining soil to prevent water accumulation.

Watering

Sparse Watering

Water sparingly, allowing the soil to dry out completely between waterings.

Pot Size

Large Pot

Choose a larger pot initially to accommodate the plant's growth.

Colors

Century plants come in various colors such as green, yellow, and red.

Benefits

Drought Tolerance

Century plants are extremely drought-resistant once established.

Architectural Beauty

They add a striking, architectural element to gardens or landscapes.

Non-Benefits

Overwatering can lead to root rot and damage for century plants.
